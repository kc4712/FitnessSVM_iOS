This exercise is for the women
who has gave a afterbirth 1~4 weeks.

The most important part of this exercise is
to start at the slow time.
Step.1 Yoga and weight training
(it is exercise that balances
the whole body structure)
it helps to make a corrective
pelvic osteotomy and strength the abdominal.
Before starting the exercise,
needs to give enough stretching,
without doing too much exercise.
Also needs to think
to learn step by step.

Mother who has done cesarean recommended to start exercise after the 2 months.
Also with the mother who has give a normal birth need to take a enough
resting before starting this exercise.
iBODY24 is the exercise which has been certificated from the obstetrics gynecology.

This exercise is for the women
who has gave an afterbirth 5~8 weeks.

It is ungraded version of Step.1;
this exercise is more focus
on the making whole body muscle with
also focusing on the balance.
Especially, its very good
for the backbone erector.
Before starting the exercise,
please do enough stretching to get
beautiful body


Other who has done cesarean recommended to start exercise after the 3~4 months.
Also with the mother who has give a normal
birth need to take a enough resting before starting this exercise.
iBODY24 is the exercise which has been certificated from the obstetrics gynecology.
